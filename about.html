    Hello, I'm Vincent van Hannen. I was born and raised in Eindhoven, the Netherlands.

    From a young age, I’ve always had an interest in video and photography. It all started with a kid’s camera, and later an action cam. I found it so fascinating that I even gave a school presentation about it. Unfortunately, during my high school years, this hobby received less attention. However, when I began studying journalism, my passion for it started to return.

    During my studies, I discovered that I really enjoyed taking photos. I began studying more about cameras and worked to take my photography to the next level. This led me to practice photography almost daily. With my Canon 250D, I aimed to capture the most beautiful images. Then, during a videography assignment for school, I decided to use this same camera and realized that I enjoyed making moving images even more than taking photos.

    Now, some time has passed. I’ve replaced my Canon 250D with a Sony A7Cii and gained more experience along the way. Feel free to browse through my portfolio and discover the stories I’ve captured. If you're interested in collaborating or have any questions, don’t hesitate to get in touch!
